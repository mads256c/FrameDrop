Framedrop?

In a game where the challenge, is to complete the game in the lowest of refresh-rates, with random lag-spikes and challenging enemies. Are you able to take the challenge in your own hands, and complete the game?

What is Framedrop?

Framedrop is an unique game for people with horrible humor. Framedrop is also an extremely buggy feature packed game!

Framedrop has features like:

  1. Lag-spikes (lots of them)
  2. Low refresh-rates
  3. Generated levels
  4. Guns, a lot of guns
  5. Achievements (note quite done yet)
  6. Innovative parkour system
  7. Bad references and jokes
  8. A great soundtrack
  9. Gaben
